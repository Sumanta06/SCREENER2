How to Read Signals
What each signal means and what action to take
▼
▲ Long Buildup
OI ↑ + Price ↑ + Volume spike. Fresh aggressive buying.
→ Bullish Trade
▼ Short Buildup
OI ↑ + Price ↓ + Volume spike. Fresh aggressive selling.
→ Bearish Trade
↑ Short Covering
OI ↓ + Price ↑. Bears exiting — can trigger sharp upward move.
→ Bullish Watch
↓ Long Unwinding
OI ↓ + Price ↓. Bulls exiting — can trigger sharp drop.
→ Bearish Watch
◆ Accumulation
OI ↑ + Price ↑ + Vol normal. Smart money quietly buying.
→ Watchlist (Long)
◆ Distribution
OI ↑ + Price ↓ + Vol normal. Smart money quietly shorting.
→ Watchlist (Short)
↔ Neutral
OI ↑ but price hasn't moved. Market in tug-of-war.
→ Skip for Now
