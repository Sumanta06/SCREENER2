📡 Rolling Compare kya hai? ▾
Har 10 minute pe worker automatically NSE se fresh data fetch karta hai aur:

• 10 min pehle ke scan se compare → signals generate
• 20 min pehle ke scan se compare → signals generate
• Agar dono mein same symbol, same signal → STRONG tag lage

Aapko kuch karne ki zaroorat nahi — Live tab automatically update hota hai.
🔥 STRONG signal kya hota hai? ▾
Agar koi symbol 10 min compare mein bhi signal de raha hai aur 20 min compare mein bhi same direction mein — to woh STRONG hai.

Matlab: 2 alag timeframes confirm kar rahe hain. Yeh highest priority trade candidates hain. WEAK sirf ek timeframe mein signal deta hai.
📊 Signal Types kya matlab hai? ▾
LONG BUILDUP
OI ↑ + Price ↑ + Volume spike — Fresh buying
SHORT COVERING
OI ↓ + Price ↑ — Bears exiting
ACCUMULATION
OI ↑ + Price ↑ (no vol spike) — Slow buying
SHORT BUILDUP
OI ↑ + Price ↓ + Volume spike — Fresh selling
LONG UNWINDING
OI ↓ + Price ↓ — Bulls exiting
DISTRIBUTION
OI ↑ + Price ↓ (no vol spike) — Slow selling
📅 Day View kaise use karein? ▾
Day View tab mein aaj ke saare rolling scans ka timeline dikhta hai:

• Green bar = us scan mein bullish signals zyada
• Red bar = bearish signals zyada
• Fire icons = us scan ke STRONG signals
• Leaderboard = kaun sa symbol aaj sabse zyada baar signal diya

Kisi bhi scan row pe tap karke us waqt ke signals dekh sakte ho.
⚙️ Setup kaise karein? ▾
1. Settings tab mein apna Cloudflare Worker URL paste karo
2. Save dabao — URL browser mein save ho jayegi
3. Test Connection se verify karo
4. Live tab pe aao — auto-refresh 10 min mein pehla result dikhayega

Manual baseline: Compare tab → Save Baseline → PIN dalo → Baseline save hoga.
